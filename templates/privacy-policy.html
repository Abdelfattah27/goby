Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you create an account, place an order, or contact us. This may include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information
  • Order history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate with you about orders and promotions
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Restaurants to fulfill your orders
  • Delivery partners to deliver your orders
  • Service providers who assist with payment processing
  • When required by law or to protect rights

4. Your Choices

You may:

  • Update your account information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@goby.com.
